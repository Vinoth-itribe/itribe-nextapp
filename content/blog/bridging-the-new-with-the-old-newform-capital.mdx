## The Challenge
Newform Capital sought to bridge the traditional financial world with the rapidly developing Web 3 finance sector. Our task was to create a brand and digital presence that communicated the complex nature of blockchain technology in a user-friendly way, appealing to both traditional financial investors and blockchain enthusiasts.

## Our Approach
We worked closely with Newform Capital to understand their business objectives and target audience. Through extensive research, we developed a brand identity, including a new logo, color palette, and typography that reflected the balance between traditional finance and Web 3 innovation. To simplify the complexities of blockchain technology, we used 60s-inspired imagery to communicate its transformative potential.
